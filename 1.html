Hi, people watching this web. I am Lee Kwan Ho who lives in South Korea. On November 17, 2022, I was in a position not to go to the university I wanted because I didn't get good grades on the exam, the most important test for entering college in Korea. So after the college entrance exam, I don't want to just play in my free time, but I'm making a web with HTml because I'm wondering if there's anything that'll help me in my life. To be honest, before I took the CSAT, I thought I could get good grades and go to the university I wanted, but it wasn't as easy as I thought. I said I'll do my best. I tried to pretend to be happy because I was nosy with my friends before taking the CSAT, but even that was not easy. There's nowhere to lament, so I'm writing here.
